About Me

Joe Tran (Curriculum Vitae)

Hello, my name is Joe. I am currently a fourth year undergraduate student at York University majoring completing my degree in (Specialized Honours BSc.) Mathematics for Education. While completing my courses as an undergraduate, I really enjoy the concepts that are taught in my classes such as Real Analysis, Complex Analysis, Topology, and many more. Currently, I am an Undergradaute Teaching Assistant taking on roles such as grading, invigilating, and even being a tutorial leader. I have been a teaching assistant for courses such as Calculus, Linear Algebra, Business Mathematics, and Problems, Conjectures, and Proofs (Tutorial Leader). I am planning to pursue a Masters degree in Pure Mathematics, and further down the road planning to pursue a PhD in Pure Mathematics as well. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions!
