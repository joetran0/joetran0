About Me

I am current a MA student in Pure Mathematics at York University, and an incoming PhD student in Applied Mathematics at York University. My current focus is on Functional Analysis and Banach Space Theory, but as I transition into Applied Mathematics, my focus will be on Dynamical Systems and Mathematical Epidemiology. My academic journey has recently shifted toward more applied directions, especially in Mathematical Epidemiology. I care deeply about education and have received highly positive feedback from students as a TA.
