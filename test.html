About Me

Joe Tran (Curriculum Vitae)

Hello, my name is Joe, and my life has been an intricate equation, with each variable representing a unique facet of my journey. Currently enrolled at York University, my academic adventure revolves around the pursuit of knowledge in mathematics, coupled with a passion for education. As a student pursuing a Specialized Honours (BSc.) in Mathematics for Education at York University, my days are filled with the beauty of numbers and the joy of imparting that knowledge to others. Mathematics isn't just a subject for me; it's a language that speaks to the very essence of the universe. I find solace in solving complex problems, unraveling mathematical mysteries, and discovering the patterns that underpin the world around us. The academic challenges of my major have not deterred me but rather fueled my desire to delve deeper into the realm of pure mathematics. My ultimate goal is to embark on the MA program at York University, where I can immerse myself in the theoretical intricacies of pure mathematics. I am driven by a thirst for understanding and a commitment to contributing to the ever-expanding tapestry of mathematical knowledge. Beyond the classroom, my interests are as diverse as the numbers I study. Teaching is not just a career path for me; it's a calling. The joy of helping others grasp the beauty of mathematics and witnessing the "aha" moments in my students is immensely rewarding. It's my way of paying forward the knowledge and inspiration I've received along my journey. On a lighter note, when I'm not engrossed in the world of mathematics, you can find me indulging in two of my favorite hobbies: watching anime and playing Pokémon GO. Anime, with its rich storytelling and diverse characters, offers me a different kind of mental stimulation. It's my escape into imaginative worlds that provide a refreshing break from the rigors of academia. Meanwhile, Pokémon GO takes me outdoors, combining my love for technology and the thrill of exploration.
